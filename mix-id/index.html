How It Works

1

Download

Give it a URL or local file. yt-dlp handles the download from any supported platform.

2

Scan

Audio gets split into segments and fingerprinted against Shazam's catalog of 100M+ songs.

3

Identify

Matches are deduplicated, timestamped, and output as a clean tracklist in TXT, CUE, and JSON.

Three output formats.

.txt

Tracklist

Paste-friendly. Perfect for Mixcloud descriptions, show notes, or social posts.

.cue

CUE sheet

Import into DJ software, media players, or audio editors with track markers.

.json

Structured data

Full metadata â€” title, artist, album, timestamps. Build on top of it.
